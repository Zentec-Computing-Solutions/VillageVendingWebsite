MPI advice

Raw milk is safe and healthy when handled correctly. Follow these basics to reduce risk.

  • Keep it chilled while transporting it home from the farm.
  • Keep raw milk in the coldest part of your fridge, usually the lower levels at 4C or less.
  • Throw it out if it has been left out for 2 or more hours.
  • Drink it by its use-by date.
  • Tell friends or visitors about the risks if you serve raw milk.
  • Buy only from a registered supplier.
