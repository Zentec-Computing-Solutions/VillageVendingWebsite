Milk Dispensers

High quality Italian built fresh milk dispensing machines, with models ranging from 200 to 1800 litre capacity, plus housing units and bottle dispensing machines.

  • Internal refrigerating cell for milk tanks, temperature and alarms.
  • 200 litre stainless steel tanks on wheels with Clean In Place options.
  • 200, 400, and 600-1800 litre capacity options.
  • Removable tanks for washing and payment options available.
  • Accurate measuring with built-in flow meter.
  • Alarm monitoring via text and email alerts.

Dispensers

Sell a wide range of products 24/7. Machines are purpose-built for safe milk and food sales, with custom shelving for packaged food and chilled or frozen goods.

  • Simple to clean and easy to use.
  • Elevator included for fragile goods.
  • Product dispensed via pushers or spirals.
  • Custom built for different product sizes.
  • 5-10 shelving options and efficient refrigeration.
  • Alarm monitoring via text and email alerts.

Consultancy

End-to-end advisory from viability assessment and scoping through to delivery, installation and ongoing implementation support. Village Vending has helped New Zealand farmers implement farm gate raw milk sales since 2012.

After Sales Support

Delivery, installation, technical training, round the clock help for technical queries, replacement parts and a large stock of parts held in New Zealand.

Bottles

Reusable bottles are a large part of the appeal of locally sourced milk. Village Vending sources easy to clean 1 litre glass bottles with secure metal lids, available by the pallet.

Other Products

Dispense dairy products, ice cream, honey, eggs, olive oil, wine, water, cured meats, fresh pasta, boxed produce and other packaged refrigerated goods.
